WELCOME TO EPIC ART STUDIO

Our primary focus is educating through fun and unique methods that engage you and leave you wanting to learn more! Acclaimed as being ranked one of the best in the nation, EPIC Art offers many courses suited for your level of comfort.

Travel through the world of art as we teach you all of the different mediums of the craft alongside our highly knowledgeable staff team.

Work your way up from the bunny hills of paints and clays to the echelons of marble sculpting, or start at the top if you're confident enough and work alongside the World-Renowned artist Gustav Fredrickson as he shows you a new side of art you've never seen before.

Whether you desire to make a career out of art or just want to pick up a new hobby to pass the time, EPIC Art is equipped and prepared to help you start your dream right. What will YOU create?

OUR PHILOSOPHY

We believe that Art is a gateway to a better, more beautiful side of the world, inspired by the dynamic and abstract way of thinking one must have to bring an idea onto the canvas. Our Art is about executing those thoughts clearly and vividly in a way that it expresses who we are, without needing an explanation as to why it came to be in the first place. Art should have no rules or limits, and here at EPIC Art we hope to inspire you to put your heart into the work that you create. We will happily refund anyone not satisfied with our services, and encourage you to leave feedback on your experiences with us so that we can continue to grow and inspire you as best as we can.
